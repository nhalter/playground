Content Promotion

Content Distribution Outreach

We’ll reach out to influencers and other key players who might be interested in sharing your article on their own social sites to expand your reach.

Social Media

Through a combination of paid and scheduled posts, we’ll apply best practices’ in social media to drive engagement and make sure your content lands right in your targeted audience’s lap.

Email

We’ll design and send out a TL;DR email that will introduce this piece of content to your current email subscribers in order to move them to your specified call to action.

Content Strategy

Research & Consultation

One of our Startup Consultants will conduct a strategy call to learn more about your business, then work with you through each step of the process. Behind the scenes, the Startup Consultant will perform keyword and influencer research to ensure that your piece of content is placed in front of the right audiences.

Customized Content Marketing Strategy

After touching base with our Startup Consultant, you will receive a Strategy Briefing detailing the target audience/persona(s), key research findings, direction of the content and a timeline for execution.

Content Creation

Professionally Developed Content Asset

Armed with your industry and a topic, our writers will craft a professional content asset, following best practices, we know are effective in reaching your target audience. Once complete, our design team will add visual intrigue striking the perfect balance of copy and design for the intended medium.

Attention-Grabbing Direct Outreach Drafts

In addition to a polished, professional piece of content, our writers will draft a set of 3 custom email pitches geared to spark social media account managers’ interest to get the conversation started. With a quick personal touch here and there, these pitches will be ready to go.

Social Post Development

We’ll research the best strategy and develop 10 social hooks that are based upon your strengths and your target market—Truly, an essential part of our content marketing platform!
