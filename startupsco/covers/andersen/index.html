Startups.co

Introduction

Fundable

Our brand is more than the Startups.co name and logo -- it’s our values and our reputation. Every encounter people have with the Startups.co brand should reinforce what we stand for, and leave them with a positive impression. These design and messaging guidelines will help you ensure all brand expressions look, feel, and sound Startups.co.

Staying true to this identity and communicating consistently across the entire Startups.co experience makes it easier to build trust and forge an authentic connection with our audiences. It’s this bond that gives us more influence over public perception, more loyal advocates, and a more valuable brand.
